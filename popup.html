How to Use TalkScribe

  • Click in any text field where you want to type.
  • Right-click and select Start Dictation.
  • Speak clearly into your microphone.
  • Your words will appear automatically in the text field.
  • Click Stop Dictation when you are done.
  • Make sure your microphone is turned on and allowed in Chrome.
TalkScribe makes typing faster by converting your voice into text.
